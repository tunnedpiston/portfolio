Company Overview

About Software Swaft

The Swift & Craft Fusion

Software Swaft is a software development studio founded and run by engineering students. We build websites, e-commerce platforms, and custom applications for small businesses, founders, and individuals who need something that works — and works well — without enterprise overhead.

Every project that leaves our hands is built, reviewed, and shipped by the same small team that scoped it. There's no layer of account managers between the brief and the build, which means decisions move fast and feedback reaches the people actually writing code.

We started by building for people around us — a family business, a friend's workshop, a local gym — before taking on clients further afield. That grounding shapes how we work: practical scopes, honest timelines, and pricing that reflects who we are.

"The name itself carries the idea: Swaft, a fusion of Swift and Craft — fast delivery, without cutting the craft out of the work."

How We Work

Development Process

Seven stages, the same for every project regardless of size — so nothing gets skipped under deadline pressure.

Step 01

Requirement Discussion

We start with a conversation — what you need, who it's for, and what success looks like at the end.

Step 02

Planning

Scope, timeline, and a fixed price are agreed before any design or code work begins.

Step 03

UI/UX Design

Wireframes and visual design, shared with you for review before development starts.

Step 04

Development

The build phase — front end, back end, and integrations, with regular progress updates.

Step 05

Testing

Cross-device and cross-browser checks, plus functional testing of every feature.

Step 06

Deployment

The site or app goes live, with domain, hosting, and any required configuration handled.

Step 07

Maintenance & Support

A defined support window after launch, for fixes, small changes, and questions.
